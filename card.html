Arabian Oryx

Oryx is sometimes considered as the specie of wild buffalo, the giant auroch that became extinct during the Mesopotamia civilization.

  • Scientific Name: Oryx leucoryx
  • Average Length: 1 m (39 in)
  • Average Lifespan: 20 Years
  • Habitat: Arabian Peninsul

Oryx is easily recognized by horse-like neck with mane, white head covered with black triangular patches and long V-shaped
